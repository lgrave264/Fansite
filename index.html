About the Show Main Character/Favorite character

My Hero Acamedia is a Anime featuring a world where everyone has superpowers. These are known as Quirks and no one will ever have the same quirk although there are quirks with similar abilities. Almost Every quirk has a down side which limits the peoples ability to use the quirk.There is a rare genetic mutation where someone can be quirkless. In this world people use there quirks for good and for bad and become Villains and super heros. We follow Izuku Midoriya, a quirkless teen whos wish more than anything in the world is to be a super hero.
